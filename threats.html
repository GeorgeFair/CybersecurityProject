Phishing

Fake emails or messages designed to steal personal information like passwords or credit card details.

Malware

Malicious software such as viruses, worms, or spyware that damages or gains access to systems.

Ransomware

A type of malware that locks your files or system and demands payment to restore access.

Social Engineering

Manipulating people into giving up confidential information through deception or trust.

Man-in-the-Middle Attack

Attackers secretly intercept communication between two parties to steal or alter data.
